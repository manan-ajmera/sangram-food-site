About Sangram Food & Products Co.

Sangram Food & Products Co. is engaged in the production and distribution of premium quality namkeen and farsan. As a local, family-managed proprietorship, we focus on authentic taste, hygienic preparation and consistent supply to our trade partners.

We serve wholesalers, retailers, caterers, canteens and walk-in customers through our retail presence in Visnagar.
